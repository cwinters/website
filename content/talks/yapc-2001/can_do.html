What can you do?

Okay, so what can you do with it?

Easily make legacy or disparate datasources available through web front end

Treat lots of different types of data in the same way (full-text search across HTML pages, news entries, bug database, hate mail to television networks, etc.)

Develop a rapid prototype for a larger system -- Bayer Plastics and Java-based system (and maybe the prototype becomes the system!)

Enable your users to edit content through a web front end

Create (yet another) web-based content management system with security already done for you

Soon: Make your data and business logic available as a service through HTTP, SOAP, etc.
