Define as you go: YAGNI

  • My mindreading trick...
  • Yes, it IS tempting to create objects for all tables from the start
  • Including the foreign keys for relationships
  • But you might not need all of the tables (O/R mismatch)
  • Plus: it's a BIG system: all at once is overwhelming
  • And the system is changing all the time (we're agile!)
