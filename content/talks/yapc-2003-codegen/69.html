Relationship Declaration

  • Keep it simple
  • Example of one-to-one relationship
[relate CustomerBalance]
type          = one
field_map     = custRscno >> cbalRscno
field_map     = custEntno >> cbalEntno
  • Example of one-to-many relationship
[relate GasContract]
type          = many
field_map     = custRscno >> gascRscno
field_map     = custEntno >> gascEntno
