Transactions

  • Canonical example: the bank
  • Not all databases support transactions
  • If your database doesn't support transactions, the DBI won't give it to you
  • If using, remember to set {AutoCommit} = 0 either in your connect method or later.
  • Remember to commit!

Transaction operations:

# Set AutoCommit to false
 $dbh->{AutoCommit} = 0;

# Commit a series of statements
 $dbh->commit();

# Rollback a series of statements
 $dbh->rollback();
