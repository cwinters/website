The statement handle

  • Similar in nature to the database handle
  • It's just a reference!
  • Create with the prepare method call to the database handle
  • After prepare succeeds, call execute on the handle
  • Use for SELECT statements and for retrieving metadata from the database
 my $sth = $dbh->prepare( $sql ) || die "Cannot prepare! $DBI::errstr";
 $sth->execute()                 || die "Cannot execute! $DBI::errstr";
