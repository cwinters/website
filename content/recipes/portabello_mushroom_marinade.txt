<h1>Portabello Mushroom Marinade</h1>

<pre>
5 T toasted sesame oil
3 T vinegar (rice or wine)
2 T tamari
2 T lemon juice
2 garlic cloves, minced

Cut small slits in mushrooms, soak for as long as you like. (I
did ~six hours and it was great.)
</pre>
